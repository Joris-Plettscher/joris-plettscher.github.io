Research Projects

Influence of Pruning on Polysemanticity in Artificial Neural Networks

Features are measurable properties of the data that are relevant to solving a certain task. When multiple unrelated features significantly impact the activation of an individual neuron in a neural network, it is called polysemanticity. This thesis aims to investigate the influence of pruning, meaning the removal of parameters from a model, on the degree of polysemanticity in neural networks.
